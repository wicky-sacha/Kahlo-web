1791-1940

The beginning of the feminist movement. Starting from Olympe de Gouges who wrote the Declaration of the Rights of Woman and the Female Citizen in 1791 we will go through the many impactful feminist who gave life to the movement as we know of today. Among these heroic women we will introduce you many writers, painters, journalists and politicians.

1940-2021

Feminism activism during World War II and after the conflict. Who are the faces of feminism during the 20th and 21st centuries? Writers (Simone de Beauvoir), painters (Frida Kahlo), and visionaries pushing women's rights forward to equality (Simone Veil). This frieze regroups every unifying events of the feminist movement throughout the 20th century in order to represent a narrative of feminism.
