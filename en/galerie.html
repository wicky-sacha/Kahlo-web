La Fronde

It was the first French journal which was completely created and directed by women (Marguerite Durand). It was only the second journal of this type in the whole world. The first one, The Woman's Era, was created by an afro-american activist Josephine St. Pierre Ruffin in 1894.

Freemasonry

Freemasonry refers to a secret society which recruits its members through co-option. Freemasons practice initiation rites following the principles of the masonic secret regarding the arts of building.

The Suffragettes

A group of women who are activists for women vote in Great-Britain and later in Europe.

Familial Planning

The Familial Planning takes in consideration every type of sexualities. It defends the right to contraceptives and abortion. It was the first militant movement regarding sexual education.

The Veil Abortion Act

Law of the 17th January 1975 which legalize abortion. Its name comes from Simonne Veil, the politician who proposed this law.

Femen

Feminist movement created in 2008 in Ukraine. This organisation fights for women’s rights. Femen activists are famous because of their provocative aspect since they often protest bare-chest.

#MeToo

This movement started in 2007 but only became mainstream in 2017 after the Weinstein allegations. #MeToo is worldwide famous. However, a lot of local variants exists according to languages and cultures such as #balancetonporc in France.
